## LCA Jump

### Functions
- `init(n)`: Initializes variables
- `gen(root)`: Generates from $root$, can easily change to vector for forests. Just repeat `gen`$ for each root. 
- `jump(x, d)`: Jump $d$ upwards from node $x$. 
- `lca(x, y)`: Lowest common ancestor of $x, y$.
